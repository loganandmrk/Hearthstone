options to view cards:

!text filter: filter through any abilities that contain a specific string
!mana cost: filter for any cards with a specific mana cost (0 - 6 individually, 7+ grouped together) 
!name: filter for any cards with a specific name string
type: filter by type
attack and health: filter by a minions attack or health


options to manage data:
add new cards to the csv
remove cards from the csv
